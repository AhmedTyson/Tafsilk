1. **Open Swagger UI**
   ```
   https://localhost:7186/swagger
   ```

2. **Register a user**
   - Click POST /api/auth/register
 - Click "Try it out"
   - Click "Execute"
   - Copy the userId from response

3. **Login**
   - Click POST /api/auth/login
   - Click "Try it out"
   - Click "Execute"
   - Copy the token from response

4. **Authorize**
   - Click "Authorize" button (top right)
   - Type: Bearer {paste-token-here}
   - Click "Authorize"
   - Click "Close"

5. **Get your profile**
   - Click GET /api/auth/me
   - Click "Try it out"
   - Click "Execute"
   - See your profile data!
